Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase tickets?

You may purchase tickets through SM Cinema website (https://www.smcinema.com) or at any SM Cinema ticket counter.

Are cashless payments accepted?

For transactions on this website, you may pay with any credit/debit card. For transactions at ticket counters, you may pay with BDO Pay, GCash, GrabPay, PayMaya, WeChatPay, Alipay, and debit/credit card (currently available in select branches).

How do I get my ticket that I purchased online?

You will receive an email confirmation upon successful purchase.

Is the ticket refundable?

Tickets purchased are strictly non-refundable and non-cancellable.

Is food allowed inside the SM Cinema?

Yes.
