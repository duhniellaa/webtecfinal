Dining

Filipino Foods

If you're craving for filipino foods, SM Seaside is the best place since they have multiple restaurants that serves homecooked-style filipino foods like Kuya J!

Korean Foods

Are you a k-drama or kpop fan? You can enjoy korean foods at SM Seaside so you can act like you're in a Korean Drama and eat like a korean!

Japanese Foods

For anime lovers who loves studio ghibli foods, you can try Japanese Menus at SM Seaside because they have multiple japanese restaurant there that serves aesthetic and delicious anime-like foods.

Buffets

Buffets are by far the best spots to eat in. Whether you're celebrating a birthday or just casual dining, SM Seaside has a lot of buffet options you can choose from..

Fastfood

If you are in a hurry you can eat at a fast food instead at SM Seaside. No need to wait for minutes so you can get to your next appointment in time!

Sugbo Mercado

Sugbo Mercado at Sky Park is the newest dining at SM Seaside. With multiple food choices, from Filipino to International taste, you can also enjoy the view while eating.

Foodcourt

SM Seaside has a very huge foodcourt across their famous Ice Skating Rink so you can definitely take break from Ice Skating and enjoy a meal at the foodcourt.

Snacks & Desserts

Beat the heat with cold snacks and desserts at SM Seaside. From shaved ice to smoothies, you can enjoy it at SM Seaside because there are a lot of stalls and restaurants that serves desserts.
