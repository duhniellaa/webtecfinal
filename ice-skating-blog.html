Unleash your inner Elsa
at SM Seaside Ice Skating Rink

May 9, 2023

Escape the heat and glide into fun at SM Seaside's Ice Skating Rink. Whether you're a pro or a beginner, enjoy the cool breeze and the adrenaline rush as you skate on the ice. Come join the fun and create unforgettable memories on the ice at SM Seaside.

Ice skating is a fun and exciting activity that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. It is a perfect way to beat the heat and experience something different. If you're looking for a great place to go ice skating, the SM Seaside City Cebu is a must-visit destination. Here are some reasons why.
The SM Seaside City Cebu is a premier shopping destination in the city. It boasts of a variety of shops, restaurants, and entertainment venues. One of its main attractions is its ice skating rink. The rink is located on the third floor of the mall and is open to the public.

The ice skating rink in SM Seaside City Cebu is modern and well-maintained. It is a great place for beginners to learn how to skate, and for experienced skaters to show off their skills. The rink is equipped with all the necessary equipment, including skates and protective gear. There are also trained staff members on hand to assist skaters if needed.
