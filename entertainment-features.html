Entertainment

Ice Skating Rink

One of the largest ice skating rink in the Philippines, SM Seaside is known for its Ice Skating rink which is very popular with children, even with Adults!

Cinema

Enjoy the modern cinema at SM Seaside with a lot of cinema types to choose from! Director's Club with recliner chairs or Large Screen Format with the largest screen you can see a movie from.

Arcades

SM Seaside is also known for the variety of arcades it has. You can play claw machines here and also enjoy childhood games like tekken, and also ticket-winning games.

Bowling

Enjoy bowling here at SM Seaside for an affordable price only! You can either play with your family or with your friends so you can have the best times together.

Kids Zone

If you have a little one, then SM Seaside is the perfect place for you. While your little ones enjoy the slides, you can just take a rest while watching over them.

Skypark

This is by far the best place at SM Seaside. You can enjoy the view here while your little ones are playing at the play area. They also have food stalls here so you can eat and enjoy the view at once.
