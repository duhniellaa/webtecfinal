SM Seaside: Home of Food and Dining

May 4, 2023

Looking for a dining destination that caters to the whole family? Look no further than SM Seaside Food Dining! Not only do they offer a diverse range of dining options to satisfy every taste, but they also have a buffet and restaurant that are perfect for families. With a variety of dishes to choose from, even picky eaters will find something they love.

SM Seaside Food and Dining is a foodie's paradise located in Cebu City, Philippines. This dining destination boasts a diverse selection of local Filipino and international cuisine, making it the perfect spot for food lovers who crave variety. With more than 100 food establishments to choose from, there's something for everyone. One of the highlights of SM Seaside Food and Dining is their buffet restaurant. With an extensive spread of dishes, diners can enjoy an all-you-can-eat experience of both local and international flavors. From freshly made sushi to classic Filipino dishes, the buffet offers a range of options for diners to indulge in. If a sit-down restaurant experience is more your style, SM Seaside Food and Dining has plenty of options to choose from. From Italian restaurants serving up freshly-made pasta to Korean BBQ joints offering up sizzling meats, there's something to satisfy every craving. The restaurants are also family-friendly, making it a great spot for a night out with the family or a casual dinner with friends.

In addition to the buffet and restaurant options, SM Seaside Food and Dining also has a variety of street food stalls, perfect for those looking for a quick snack or bite to eat. From classic Filipino street food like isaw and balut to international options like takoyaki and dumplings, there's something to suit every taste bud. SM Seaside Food and Dining also boasts a colorful and vibrant ambiance that is sure to impress. The interiors of the food establishments are well-decorated and designed, providing a fun and lively atmosphere for diners to enjoy. It's a great spot for both locals and tourists alike, making it a must- visit destination in Cebu City. Overall, SM Seaside Food and Dining is a fantastic dining destination that offers a diverse range of options for every taste and budget. From the buffet and restaurant options to the street food stalls and colorful ambiance, there's something for everyone to enjoy. So come on down and indulge in a culinary journey you won't soon forget!

They also have a huge foodcourt with variety of foods to choose from. If your friends or family have different taste then the foodcourt is the best place for you! Their foodcourt is very spacious and has a lot of table to sit in, whether you are alone or with a group.
